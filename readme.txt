== Description ==

ABMsense helps businesses identify website visitors and predict similar organizations to drive B2B pipeline growth. 
The plugin provides accurate visitor insights directly to your inbox, eliminating the need for complicated dashboards or software. 
It’s perfect for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) looking to enhance their account-based marketing strategies. 
ABMsense is simple to install, free to use, and integrates seamlessly with your WordPress site. 
With AI-driven predictions, it helps you convert website traffic into qualified leads.

= Key Features: =

- One-click installation for quick setup.
- Identifies business visitors for detailed insights.
- Daily visitor intelligence sent to inbox.
- No dashboards, all insights via email.
- AI-driven predictions for high-quality leads.
- Boosts B2B sales pipeline through insights.
- Export data in CSV for analysis.
- Built-in GDPR compliance for privacy protection.
- Lead scoring to prioritize qualified leads.
- Intelligence leads for high marketing ROI.
- *Coming soon*: Integration with popular CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot.


== Installation ==

1. Go to your WordPress Dashboard.
2. Navigate to *Plugins > Add New*.
3. Search for *ABMsense*.
4. Click *Install Now*, then *Activate*.
5. Configure the plugin settings by going to the ABMsense settings page.
6. Enter your email to start receiving daily visitor insights directly to your inbox.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I install the ABMsense plugin? =
- You can install ABMsense directly from your WordPress dashboard by navigating to *Plugins > Add New* and searching for *ABMsense*. 
Then, click *Install Now* and activate the plugin.

= Is ABMsense free to use? =
- *Yes*, ABMsense is completely free to use.

= Does ABMsense store my visitor data? =
- No, ABMsense does not store any visitor data on external servers. 
All data is sent directly to your inbox and remains private.

= Can I export the visitor data? =
- Yes, ABMsense allows you to export visitor data in CSV format for easy analysis.

= Does ABMsense comply with GDPR regulations? =
- Yes, ABMsense is fully compliant with GDPR and protects user privacy.

= How do I receive visitor insights? =
- Once the plugin is installed, daily insights will automatically be sent to your email address.

= Can I customize the visitor tracking settings? =
- Yes, ABMsense allows you to customize tracking settings to suit your business needs.

= Is CRM integration available? =
- Not yet, but *coming soon*: integration with popular CRMs like Salesforce and HubSpot.

= Does ABMsense use 3rd party tools? =
- Yes, ABMsense leverages API, AI LLM technologies, and IP analysis solutions to provide comprehensive and accurate information.

= Is ABMsense compatible with all WordPress themes and plugins? =
- Yes, ABMsense is designed to be compatible with a wide range of WordPress themes and plugins. 
If you encounter any issues, our support team is here to help.

= What data will I receive in my reports? =
- You’ll receive a CSV file with 15 key parameters, including information about the visitor’s organization, location, and behavior on your site.

= Does ABMsense require any technical knowledge to set up? =
- Not at all! ABMsense is designed for easy, one-click deployment. 
Simply install the plugin, configure your email preferences, and start receiving insights without any technical hassle.
